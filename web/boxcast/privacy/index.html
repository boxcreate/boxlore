Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 4, 2026

1. Our Core Promise

boxcast is a zero-monetary-gain, exploratory pet project. We built it to learn and to share something useful with podcast listeners — not to monetize your attention or your data.

ANONYMITY FIRST

We do not know who you are. We do not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or precise GPS locations. Your app generates a random, anonymous Instance ID that is never linked to you or your device hardware.

WE NEVER
  • Use data for advertising — 0 ads, forever
  • Sell data to third parties
  • Profile or track you across apps
  • Use third-party analytics SDKs like Google Analytics or Facebook

2. Data We Collect

We use a self-hosted telemetry system that runs on our own infrastructure. Your data never touches Google, Facebook, or any third-party analytics provider.

Our Stack: Cloudflare Workers → Turso (libSQL)
All data stays in our own database. No third-party SDKs are involved.

Daily Heartbeats

A random, app-generated Instance ID and the date you last opened the app. This helps us understand how many people use boxcast — nothing more.

Usage Aggregates

Aggregated counters like "total plays today" or "total searches today," grouped by cohort (e.g. app version). These are not tied to individual users — they are summed across all users for a given day.

Podcast Intelligence

We log which podcasts are being played to eventually build native, community-driven charts. This data is strictly aggregated by podcast ID and is absolutely never linked back to you or your device.

3. What We Don't Collect

  • No PII: No names, emails, phone numbers, or accounts.
  • No Device Identifiers: No IMEI, Android ID, or advertising ID. Your Instance ID is randomly generated by the app and can be reset at any time.
  • No Location Data: No GPS, no IP-based geolocation logging.
  • No Listening History: Your subscriptions, downloads, and play history are stored only on your device and never leave it.

4. Control & Deletion

Reset Your Identity

You can regenerate your anonymous Instance ID at any time from Settings → Data & Privacy → Data Management. This acts as a "Forget Me" — your old ID becomes orphaned data.

Request Full Deletion

Email us your Instance ID and we will permanently delete all associated data.

  1. Go to Settings → Data & Privacy
  2. Expand Data Management → Request Deletion
  3. Email your Instance ID to privacy@aswin.cx

Retention Policy

  • Auto-Deletion: User-level data (heartbeats) is automatically deleted after 14 months of inactivity.
  • Aggregated Data: Daily aggregates and podcast intelligence are anonymized counters and do not contain any user-identifiable information.

5. Open Source & Verification

100% OPEN SOURCE

The entire boxcast application — including the telemetry engine, the analytics worker, and this privacy page — is fully open source. You don't have to take our word for it.
